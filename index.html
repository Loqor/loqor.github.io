Experience So Far...

Hello there! I am a developer, 3D artist, musician and a big fan of working in a workshop with CNC, anything with wood, and electronics. I used to be big in video games and the like, but I've become less avid in the last few years after, y'know, the COVID pandemic and I have more or less begun to focus more on the physical things, mainly the creational aspect. I do 3D printing and I have a massive interest in additive manufacturing, and I love making things for people, as long as I have the motivation, materials (on hand or the person pays for whatever I might need) and interest enough. A few of my interests also include theatre, cultural anthropology, religion and philosophy. I was born and raised in the Middle East (yes, I am fullblown American, just strange parental decisions), so I've always held an interest for Arab culture as well.
